What is COVID‑19?

Coronoavirus disease(COVID-19) is a highly contagious disease that is caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus.

Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Runny Nose
  • Headache
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Diarrhoea
  • Fatigue
